## Research Units

Archive of Unverified Claims of Meaning
: Stores insights such as "The manager wanted it that way" and "The package is alive with us."

Center for Digital Esotericism
: Studying progress bars, fan psalms, and mystical file names.

Chair of Applied Contextualism
: Research focus: Looking at things, remaining silent for a very long time, then claiming it was intentional.

Department for Contextual Risk Assessment
: Determines the "hazard level of situations that are actually harmless."
: Example: "Restarting a laptop just before the end of the workday" → Level 4 (mental collapse).

Department of Everyday Metaphysics
: Exploring the metaphysical meaning of Amazon packages, coffee machines, forgotten USB drives, and doorstops.

Institute for Bureaucratic Phenomenology
: Specializing in forms, ladders, traffic lights, printer errors, and anything that causes delays.
